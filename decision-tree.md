# 🌳 Decision Tree

## Structure

```
Start
│
├── Mood = Good
│   ├── High Productivity & Low Stress → Growth Day
│   └── Low Productivity → Improvement Day
│
├── Mood = Neutral
│   └── Medium Productivity → Maintenance Day
│
└── Mood = Bad
    ├── High Stress → Recovery Day
    └── Low Productivity → Warning Day
```
